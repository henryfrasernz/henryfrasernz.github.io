Lights. Cameras. Bikes!

 

We're on a mission -sharing the best cycling short films from around the world with you at the Waiheke Island Cinema.

 

The Big Bike Film Night treats pedal punters with a collection of movies that has everything a cycling centric audience could want- action, drama, humour and plenty of inspiration; it’s going to be unashamedly bike-a-liscious.

 

The short films are a great reminder of why we love to ride our bike; with stories that captivate us, stories that make us think and most importantly of all- stories that inspire us to get on a bike.

 

So… listen to the voice inside you; it’s telling you to go. Grab your mates, grab your tickets, go on… grab your bike and come along for the ride!

 

Ticket Bookings are made by visiting the Cinema or calling them to reserve your ticket on 09 372 4240.
